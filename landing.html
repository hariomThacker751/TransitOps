Core Engine

Built on rules, not guesswork

Every dispatch passes through a 13-check validation engine before a single wheel turns. No spreadsheets, no manual cross-referencing, no double-booked trucks.

13-Rule Dispatch Engine

Intelligent eligibility checks before every trip dispatch — license validity, vehicle status, cargo limits, and double-assignment prevention, all in one pass.

Atomic Status Sync

Vehicle and driver statuses update together, atomically, in a single database transaction. Dispatch a trip and both flip to "On Trip" — or neither does.

Maintenance Locking

Vehicles in maintenance are automatically blocked from dispatch. The engine checks active maintenance records before allowing any trip to go out.

Live KPI Analytics

ROI, cost breakdown, fleet utilization — all live from the database. No exports, no stale snapshots, just real numbers the moment you load the dashboard.
